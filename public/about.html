About UrbanEats

We believe everyone deserves access to amazing food, delivered fast and fresh. That's why we're revolutionizing food delivery.

Our Story

UrbanEats was founded in 2020 with a simple mission: connect hungry people with their favorite local restaurants. What started as a small idea in a dorm room has grown into a platform serving thousands of customers across 50+ neighborhoods.

We've learned that great food is more than just sustenance—it's an experience. Every order we deliver represents our commitment to quality, speed, and customer satisfaction.

Our Values
